How Billionaires Think About This

  1. Leverage, not labor. Every hour should produce output that compounds. Building a template that closes 10 clients > building 1 custom agent.
  2. Sell the result, not the hours. "We handle your phones 24/7 and you never miss a booking" > "We build AI voice agents."
  3. Speed of iteration > perfection. Ship the golf package this week. Refine after 3 clients.
  4. Say no to everything that isn't the $100K path. Static websites for $2K? No. One-off automations? No. Anything without recurring revenue? No.
  5. Risk = what you can't undo. Monthly retainers are low risk. Hiring 3 full-timers at $15K/mo burn is high risk. We don't hire. We automate.
  6. Move fast on: proven demand, warm intros, anything with less than 48hr payback on time invested.
  7. Say no to: unproven verticals, "exposure" deals, equity splits with strangers, anything requiring more than $500 before validation.
